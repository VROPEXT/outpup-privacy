Privacy Policy – OutPup

Last updated: April 13, 2026

OutPup ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the OutPup mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location data: Precise GPS location is collected while you use the walk tracking feature. Location data is only collected when the app is in use and with your explicit permission.
  • Account information: Email address used to create and manage your account.
  • Dog profiles: Name, breed, and other details you provide about your dog(s).
  • Walk data: GPS coordinates, distance, duration, and timestamps of your walks.
  • Device identifiers: Anonymous user ID generated for your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the walk tracking features of the app.
  • To display your walk history, statistics, and leaderboards.
  • To manage your account and subscription (Premium).
  • To calculate and display community rankings by city.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with the following third-party services solely to operate the app:

  • Supabase: Secure cloud database for storing your account data and walk history.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for Premium subscriptions. We do not store payment card details.

4. Data Security

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

OutPup is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking at any time via your device settings.
  • Cancel your Premium subscription at any time via Google Play.

8. Location Permissions

OutPup requests access to your device's location only while the app is in use, to track your dog walks. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings. The app will still function without location permission, but walk tracking will not be available.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: outpup.app@gmail.com

This privacy policy applies to the OutPup mobile application available on Google Play Store.
